FEATURES

BUILD FORMS AND REUSE EXISTING QUESTIONS

Easily create allkinds of survey forms through our intuitive yet powerful tool. With the help of our question library you can also easily re-use questions or entire sections from your previous forms when preparing for new surveys.

Easy to use

The formbuilder was built from the ground up to be simple and easy for anyone to use. It doesn't require any technical knowledge or advanced training. Yet, it also allows for building the most complex and advanced forms. This means it is meant for anyone from a first time user to the most advanced survey administrator. 

Powerful and compatible

Under the hood CiiYUN uses the extremely versatile XLSForm standard. This means our forms are 100% compatible with other popular tools. It also means our forms can be exported to and imported from Excel for advanced technical users doing batch manipulation. 

Question Library

Good survey forms are often built by re-using tried and true questions from previous surveys. Our question library makes this process easy and straightforward. This feature is being further expanded in the coming months to allow sharing entire question libraries with colleagues or even make them available publicly. 

If you have any questions about building forms or need help, please visit our support pages.

FEATURES

COLLECT DATA

Quickly andreliably collect your survey data on any device, online or offline. Our Android app, based on OpenDataKit, uses the power and experience of of hundreds of thousands of interviews conducted all over the world. Our webforms integration, based on Enketo, also allows collecting data on iPhones, laptops, or any other device.

Online/Offline

Our tools were built for the most demanding contexts where Internet connectivity is the exception, not the rule. Surveys can be conducted entirely while being offline, regardless what kind of device is being used during the collection process. 

Data Integrity

Collected information is stored safely. Synchronization via SSL ensures that your data can't be read by a third party. Strong safeguards exist also for avoiding data loss even during very long interviews.

Easy Synchronization

Uploading data is straightforward and can take place on demand or in the background whenever Internet connectivity kind is available. 

 

If you have any questions about collecting data or need help, please visit our support pages.

 

分析和管理数据

在收集数据后立即检查数据，或者以Excel，CSV，KML和其他数据格式下载数据，从而进行更高级的分析。

FEATURES

ANALYZE AND MANAGE DATA

Inspect your data moments after it was collected - or download it for advanced analysis in other software. CiiYUN includes simple tools for creating tables, summarizing indicators - and of course a map showing all of your collected GPS coordinates. 

Quick Analysis

  • Create summary tables for all indicators without having to download the data
  • Summarize using simple frequencies or calculating averages (mean, median, and mode)

Mapping

  • Immediately see colected points
  • Disaggregate data points by survey responses (such as gender or education level)
  • Download all GPS points as KML file to integrate into other software

Downloading data

  • Download all your data and in Excel, CSV, SPSS, and other formats
  • Download all collected media (images, videos, sound recordings) as a ZIP file
  • Data can also be accessed by other means through a robust API

If you have any questions about managing and exporting collected data or need help to get started, please visit our support pages.

关于采云(CiiYUN)

CiiYUN是全球数以万计的环报学者、卫生工作者、研究人员和企业公司使用的数据采集工具。 CiiYUN是一套可在多种环境中使的数据采集工具。我们的软件对公益事务优惠使用。CiiYUN被广泛用于自然资源保护，人道主义危机工作，以及发展中国家的援助项目等。

迅速收集准确的数据是信息提取、制定应对措施的关键步骤，尤其是在各种自然灾害之后，是抢险救灾的关键环节。CiiYUN是一个综合在线、离线功能的数据采集工具,利用移动设备和网络浏览器可以随时随地采集数据、汇总数据、发布数据，以应对各种高时效数据采集需求。

CiiYUN软件的推出，使电子数据收集更加标准，更可靠，更易于在各种各种环境中使用。软件还在不断深入开发，许多新颖的功能不断的被添加到新的版本中。如果您有任何问题或建议，请随时与我们联系。
